Art Electives

These courses look like fun!

Prerequisites:

Breathing and a pulse

Course Description

An introduction to the vast world of art. Students will learn the basics of one of the toughest art forms to master - finger painting. We will spend the first 5 weeks of the course learning the proper hand and finger techniques before we actually put paint on a piece of paper. The final exam will involve painting a snowman.

Prerequisites:

Must receive at least a C in ART 1001

Course Description

This course will provide you with an in-depth knowledge of how to create a sculpture out of Play-Doh. Prepare to get your hands dirty while making sculptures out of one of the most versatile materials in the world. The final will involve creating a model of the moon.

Prerequisites:

Must receive a C in ART 1010

Course Description

This course completes the trifecta of art courses offered at UMSL. Think the first two courses were challenging? Then college is probably not for you. This course will involve creating works of art out of crayons. The final exam will be to color a page from a coloring book.
